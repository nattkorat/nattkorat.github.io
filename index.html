About Me

Hi! I'm Natt KORAT. I received my B.A. in Psychology from the Royal University of Phnom Penh in 2022 and my B.S. in Computer Science from AGA Institute in 2023. I'm currently pursuing my M.S. in Computer Science and serves as a Lecturer and Research Assistant at the Cambodia Academy of Digital Technology (CADT). My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, natural language processing, and large language models.
